Reasons for the boom in Pre-owned Car industry

Frequent introduction of new models of cars by most of the competing Indian and global car makers in India has resulted in fast replacement cycles.

Decreasing prices and aggressive sales promotions of various car makers motivate existing car owners to sell their used cars and acquire new cars.

Entry of global car makers into the Indian car industry and increasing competition in the domestic market created a huge resource of pre-owned and new cars.
